Our Story

A Legacy Built on
the Power of Ideas

Since our founding in 1993, the Ideal Debating Club has stood as one of Bangladesh's foremost arenas of critical discourse — shaping generations of thinkers, leaders and eloquent voices.

Born from a conviction that ideas — articulated with precision and passion — can change the world, IDC has cultivated an environment where young minds are challenged to think deeper, argue sharper, and listen more keenly. For over three decades we have transformed raw potential into polished excellence.

Our members have represented Bangladesh on national and international stages, carrying the torch of rigorous debate to the highest platforms and returning to mentor the next generation — ensuring the tradition endures without compromise.

01
Critical Thinking
We cultivate the ability to analyze complex issues from multiple perspectives, question assumptions and construct rigorous evidence-based arguments.
02
Eloquent Expression
Through systematic training and competition, members develop commanding presence, clarity of speech and the art of persuasion at its finest.
03
Intellectual Community
IDC is more than a club — it is a brotherhood of curious minds, elevated through shared growth, spirited discourse and mutual respect.
04
Leadership & Legacy
We forge leaders who are not merely capable but visionary — alumni who carry the IDC standard into every field they enter.
 What We Do 

Club Activities & Programs

From weekly training sessions to national tournaments, IDC keeps its members in a constant state of intellectual growth.

Weekly Debate Sessions
Regular practice rounds in British Parliamentary, Asian and other formats to sharpen argument structure, delivery and rebuttals.
National Tournaments
Representing IDC at prestigious inter-school and inter-university competitions across Bangladesh, consistently earning top honours.
Workshops & Training
Expert-led workshops on research methodology, public speaking, logical fallacies and motion analysis for members at all levels.
International Participation
Sending delegates to international forums including World Schools and Asia-Pacific debate circuits, raising Bangladesh's global voice.
Hosting Tournaments
Organising and managing some of the country's most respected debating tournaments, setting standards for excellence in competition.
Alumni Network
A thriving network of former members across law, media, politics and academia who actively mentor current debaters and open doors.

Our Record

Three Decades of
Distinguished Achievement

National Championships
IDC has consistently dominated national debating circuits, claiming top positions at Bangladesh National Debate championships across multiple decades and disciplines.
International Representation
Our members have represented Bangladesh at international forums, including World Schools format competitions and Model UN conferences across Asia.
Alumni in National Leadership
Former IDC members hold distinguished positions in law, politics, media, academia and civil service — a testament to the transformative power of debate education.
